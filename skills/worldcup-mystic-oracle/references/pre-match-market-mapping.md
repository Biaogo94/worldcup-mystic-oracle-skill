# Pre-Match Market Mapping

Use this file after Qi Men, kit, bazi, reality inputs, and current market/lottery availability are collected, but before selecting scores or betting styles.

Core rule:

`主盘判胜负，门路判进球，盘路判让球，节奏判半全场，细节判比分。`

Do not let one symbolic signal affect every market. Assign each signal to its most natural market first. The goal is to translate a match script into coherent markets, not to buy every possible story.

Intuition rule:

`第一念定候选，逻辑定边界，赔率定金额。`

The first impression, chart image, or omen may decide which candidate score/total-goals tail receives a bounded weight boost, but it must not create a market selection that contradicts the established result, handicap, goal-channel, and tempo buckets.

## Five Signal Buckets

Classify every major signal into one bucket:

| Bucket | Chinese | Primary Market | Examples |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| result qi | 胜负气 | 胜平负 | home/away stem strength, coach authority, command advantage |
| handicap qi | 盘路气 | 让球胜平负 | domination gap, strong-vs-weak mismatch, expansion or one-goal limit |
| goal-channel qi | 门路开闭 | 总进球, both-teams-to-score narrative | open/life/scenery doors, goalkeeper clash, defensive leak |
| tempo qi | 节奏先后 | 半全场 | early pressure, slow start, late awakening, fade risk |
| image qi | 细节成像 | 比分固定 | exact structure after result, goal channel, and tempo are set |

If a signal could belong to multiple buckets, choose the bucket closest to the football action:

- Weak-side scoring signal belongs to `goal-channel qi` first, not `result qi`.
- Strong-side command belongs to `result qi` first, then `handicap qi` if expansion signals exist.
- Door blockage belongs to `goal-channel qi`; use it for totals before exact scores.
- Coach support belongs to `result qi` and `tempo qi`, not exact score by itself.
- Market odds are a reality input and script clue, not proof. Use them to check coherence and value, not to override missing football facts.

## Consensus Trap Detector

Before final score selection, identify whether the market/commentary consensus is too narrow.

A consensus trap is active when at least two conditions hold:

- Public analysis clusters around one middle script such as `1:0`, `2:1`, `强队小胜`, `沉闷小球`, or `大热艰难过关`.
- The favorite still has a clear squad, tactical, Qi Men, or key-person edge.
- Handicap prices or commentary make `让胜` feel emotionally unattractive even though football logic supports a two-goal route.
- High-goal or `胜其它` prices are unusually large because the middle script is overcrowded.
- The underdog has a weak-side scoring signal that raises total goals without necessarily changing the result.

When active, do not answer with only narrow score wrapping. Run the `Strong Favorite Expansion Trigger` and `Anti-Consensus Barbell Upgrade` checks.

## Strong Favorite Expansion Trigger

Before final score selection, check whether a favorite should be moved from narrow win to expansion win.

Expansion trigger is active when at least three conditions hold:

- Reality gap: stronger squad, deeper bench, ranking/federation gap, or a clear tactical mismatch.
- Qi Men attack support: 开门, 生门, 景门, 九天, 值符, 天英, 天冲, or a favorable palace generating the favorite.
- Favorite key-person support: coach, primary striker, primary creator, or impact substitute has useful bazi support.
- Underdog defense/GK warning: goalkeeper clash, defensive leader clash, 天芮/死门/惊门/玄武/腾蛇 affecting defensive structure.
- Kit support: favorite kit is generated by seasonal/hour/palace qi or restrains underdog attacking colour.
- Market coherence: the favorite win is short enough to be the result anchor, while `让胜` or high-goal options still carry meaningful payout.

When active:

- Raise handicap expectation from `让平/让负` toward `让胜/让平`.
- Raise score structures from `1:0/2:1` toward `2:0/3:1/4:1`.
- Raise total-goal band by one step if the underdog also has a scoring signal.
- Include a small high-odds tail such as `胜其它` or total goals `5/6/7+` when available.

If only two conditions hold, keep expansion as a secondary path, not the main path.

## Weak-Side Goal Classifier

If the weaker team shows a scoring signal, classify the goal before changing the result call:

| Weak Goal Type | Conditions | Market Effect |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 偷一球 | underdog has goal-channel qi but weak result qi | keep favorite win; raise totals; use `2:1/3:1/4:1` |
| 追平球 | underdog has goal-channel qi plus draw/blocked favorite signals | add draw protection; use `1:1/2:2` |
| 反超球 | underdog has result qi, open/life gate, and favorite GK/defense clash | add upset path; use `0:1/1:2` |
| 垃圾时间球 | favorite expansion trigger active and underdog goal comes from late/chaos signal | favorite handicap may still win; use `3:1/4:1/4:2` |

Never equate "underdog can score" with "favorite cannot cover" unless draw or upset conditions are also active.

## Score Structure Tree

Select a score structure before selecting exact scores:

| Structure | Conditions | Scores | Totals |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 零封强胜 | favorite result qi + expansion trigger, underdog goal-channel weak | `2:0`, `3:0`, `4:0` | `2/3/4` |
| 强胜但丢球 | favorite result qi + expansion trigger + underdog scoring signal | `2:1`, `3:1`, `4:1`, `4:2` | `3/4/5/6` |
| 僵持小胜 | favorite result qi but no expansion, blocked doors or low tempo | `1:0`, `2:1` | `1/2/3` |
| 平局结构 | both result qi close, blocked favorite, or draw signs | `0:0`, `1:1`, `2:2` | `0/2/4` |
| 客胜反击 | away result qi or favorite defensive collapse | `0:1`, `1:2`, `0:2` | `1/2/3` |
| 混乱大球 | injury/card/chaos plus open doors | `3:2`, `2:3`, `胜其他`, `负其他` | `5/6/7+` |

The score pool must match the selected structure. Do not mix structures unless the report explicitly names a main structure and a protective structure.

## Intuition-To-Market Mapping

After the formal score structure is chosen, map any usable intuition to the narrowest market it naturally supports:

| Intuition Image | Market First | Scenario Adjustment |
| --- | --- | --- |
| one clean break / shutout image | 比分 then TTG | boost `1:0`, `2:0`, `0:1`, or `0:2`; do not widen score pool |
| flood / open road / repeated pressure | HHAD then TTG | boost expansion scores and total goals `4/5/6/7+` |
| locked gate / stuck rhythm / heavy field | TTG then draw score | boost low totals and `0:0/1:1` protect branch |
| late lightning / sudden reversal | 半全场 then result protection | small boost to late-change path only |
| chaotic noise / card / injury sign | TTG and tail CRS | boost high-goal or `胜其它/负其它`; lower exact middle scores |
| cold sensation against favorite | HHAD protection then draw/upset | add or boost protect branch; do not erase main edge unless facts also turn |

Only use an intuition image when it is recorded before final stake optimization. If it appears after seeing the optimizer's preferred long shot, treat it as rationalization and ignore it.

## Market Priority Selector

Before writing betting strategy, rank markets from best to worst for the current match:

| Match Type | Preferred Markets | Avoid or Reduce |
| --- | --- | --- |
| strong favorite expansion | 胜平负, 让球, 总进球, small high-goal tail | heavy draw protection, tiny low-score pools |
| favorite with weak-side goal | 胜平负, 总进球, 比分 `2:1/3:1/4:1` | treating weak goal as automatic draw |
| low-tempo balance | 总进球, small score pool, no-play option | aggressive handicap and wide score wrapping |
| comeback or late-awakening script | 半全场, 胜平负, totals | exact score overconcentration |
| high chaos | 总进球, small symbolic scores, no-play option | large exact-score staking |
| genuine upset | draw/upset protection, handicap against favorite | forcing a favorite anchor |

State the top two or three preferred markets in the report and explain why the reduced markets are reduced.

## Barbell Allocation Gate

Use this gate before recommending the `60/20/10/10` attack-defense model from `betting-strategies.md`.

Eligible:

- Favorite result qi is clear and final confidence is at least `medium`.
- Main-result odds can plausibly recover the plan cost or most of it.
- Expansion or chaos tail is live through at least one factual/football signal, not only imagination.
- Actual lottery terminal supports the tail option (`胜其它`, total goals `5/6/7+`, or comparable available branch).

Not eligible:

- Favorite edge is thin, lineups are missing near kickoff, or confidence is below `medium`.
- The only reason for the tail is a previous famous result; no current-match signal supports it.
- Main result odds are too low to function as an anchor and the user wants a low-risk plan.
- The plan would spend more on exact scores than on result/handicap/totals combined.

If not eligible, downgrade to conservative/no-play or present the barbell only as a replay/theoretical mapping.

## Pre-Match Conflict Check

Before finalizing, reject or rewrite any contradictory strategy:

- If main score is `3:1` or `4:1`, do not make `让负` the primary handicap pick.
- If main score is `1:0`, do not make total goals `4/5` a primary pick.
- If underdog signal is only `偷一球`, do not make draw protection larger than favorite-win allocation.
- If no tempo signal exists, do not lead with 半全场剧情型.
- If expansion trigger is active, include at least one expansion score or a total-goals tail.
- If intuition boosts a tail, confirm the same tail is allowed by at least one formal signal bucket; otherwise remove the boost.
- If official lineup is missing, avoid calling any fixed score a hard pick.
- If the strategy says "保本", show the arithmetic and the exact condition; otherwise call it a cost-control anchor.

## Bazi Role Weight Adjustment

Use bazi as a market modifier:

- Head coach: result qi and tempo qi. Default 35% of bazi judgement.
- Goalkeeper: goal-channel qi and score structure. Default 20%.
- Primary striker: goal-channel qi and expansion trigger. Default 15%.
- Primary creator or central midfielder: tempo qi and expansion trigger. Default 15%.
- Captain/defensive leader: result qi and goal-channel defense. Default 10%.
- Impact substitute: late tempo and expansion/chaos path. Default 5%.

If official lineup is unavailable, keep coach and likely goalkeeper scoring, but cap player-specific bazi confidence according to `prematch-data-status.md`.
